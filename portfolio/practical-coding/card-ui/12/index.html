Monkey is a common name that may refer to most mammals of the infraorder Simiiformes, also known as the simians.

Sheep #02

Lion #03

The lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India.

Kangaroo #04

The kangaroo is a marsupial from the family Macropodidae. In common use the term is used to describe the largest species from this family.
